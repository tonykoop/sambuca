Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic seated playing scene of the sambuca held between the player's thighs, the boat hull projecting forward, soundboard facing upward toward the player, and the neck rising away from the torso. Both hands pluck the string field symmetrically while the keel sound port points forward and downward toward listeners. Warm natural light in a quiet studio, cradle stand nearby, inlay and brass collar visible, no cultural-costume staging or fake museum labels.

## Detail Prompt

Include crisp knots, polished wood, tiny tool marks, and the brass collar softly blurred in the background. Shallow depth of field, high detail.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, fake cuneiform inscriptions, museum reproduction labels, guitar-style bridge bars, extra limbs, distorted hands, and fantasy harp proportions unrelated to the boat-shaped arched-harp design.
